Principal Desk

" We want that education by which character is formed, strength of mind is increased, the intellect is expanded, and by which one can stand on one's own feet."
- Swami Vivekananda.

Dear Friend, As we set into the new millennium the ICT industries are gaining great importance. Globalization has brought into focus the need for efficient resource utilization in a competitive environment. This has severely challenged the conventional manufacturing industries while opening up numerous avenues for service industries. In both cases Advanced Automation, Telecommunication and Computer Services are the driving forces silently causing a revolution in all walks of life. There is a great demand in the country and abroad for ICT & ITES personnel both in service sector as well as entrepreneurship.

Computer training offers you a golden chance to start a computer shop to serve community through computer services in Accounts, Desk Top Publishing, Computer Aided Design and Manufacturing or simply letter writing, data base creation etc. The field is ever expanding just like our universe and we want new stars to be discovered from amongst our students.

At Ramakrishna Mission we don't compete with anyone but only try to set up quality standards which demand enlightenment from our students. Let us proceed together to get this great country of ours with a hoary past on a blazing future course.

-- Swami Gunakarananda
Belur Math 1st May 2019
