Who We Are

RKMSCC

 

A century ago, Swami Vivekananda, the great disciple of Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa and the founder of Ramakrishna Math Mission, from his absolute realization had set prolific education standards for the building of a better nation.

He put forward noble, practical, and novel ideas about education where he laid emphasis on science & religion. He was greatly concerned with the education standards of India in comparison with the western countries. He wanted his country to be strong in science, technology as also in religion. He believed that a balanced implementation of the trio would bring moral and industrial renaissance & remove poverty. >>>

Mission & Vision


Considering India as an emerging superpower in the comity of nations, to equip the country with enlightened citizens-morally upright, intellectually sharp, emotionally balanced and technically competent.


To train young men of Bengal in state-of-the-art computer software & hardware in answer to need-based requirements, while cultivating moral & intellectual strength through co-curricular activities like Vedic prayers, Quiz contests, Games & Sports, etc.
